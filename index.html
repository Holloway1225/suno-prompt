How it works
Paste finished lyrics. The engine reads them — theme, emotional arc, density, structure — and builds three things:

Sound Prompt (full) — a rich production brief: genre, BPM feel, instruments, vocal style, dynamics, texture. No band or artist names ever.

Short Prompt — under 1000 characters, comma-tagged, drops straight into Suno's style box.

Suno-Ready Lyrics — your lyrics cleaned for Suno: consistent section tags, optional vocal cues ([whisper], [screamed], [breakdown]) where the lyric implies them, nothing reworded.

Engine — Llama is fast and free. Claude reads the lyric's nuance deepest. Everything is copyable, and every run is saved to History on this device.
